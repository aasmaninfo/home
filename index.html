History

Once an exclusive attire of princesses, the Khada Dupatta has now become a cherished ensemble embraced by brides and bridesmaids from all walks of life. This regal outfit, adorned by the royal women of Hyderabad, has evolved over the years while retaining its traditional charm.

The Khada Dupatta is a quintessential part of Hyderabadi culture and heritage. It has transitioned from being worn by women of noble backgrounds to being embraced by Hyderabadi Muslim brides on their special day. The ensemble captures attention with its elegant embroidery, incorporating elements like Gota edges, vintage jhalar/kiran, hand-embossed lampi, and hand-crafted almas, embellished with stones, Kundan, sequins, and zircons.

At Aasman, we celebrate this majestic attire and continue the legacy of the Khada Dupatta. We honour the traditional roots while infusing contemporary elements to create unique pieces. Experience the beauty and grace of the Khada Dupatta, tailored to perfection, as we bring your vision to life.

Discover the allure of this timeless tradition at Aasman - Where Elegance Meets Tradition.
